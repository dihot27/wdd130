There are alternatives to violence

One of the keys to happy and lasting relationships is the ability to handle conflict.One of the questions to ask is:Am I more concerned with "us-preservation" or more with "self-preservation"? Answer the following questions;Do you avoid people you are in conflict with?do you give up on the people you are in conflict with?do you attack the people you are in conflict with?do you solve conflict by hurting others?If you have answered yes to any of the questions,you are on the path to distructive conflict.

Do not hurt the people you love.Have emotional intelligence

Conflict is inevitable but contention is a choice
